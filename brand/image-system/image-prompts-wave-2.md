## F · TikTok story-beat scenes (one image per HookReel beat)

The `HookReel` template (`content-studio/`) takes **one background per beat** — so a hook video is a mini visual story (pain → photo → relief), a new scene every ~1.5s. We are NOT limited to the Pencil/Glow library: generate a fresh 9:16 Midjourney scene per beat. This is the storytelling engine.

**Recipe per beat:** `[who + what, matching the caption] + [home setting + light] + large warm glossy eyes + warm coral cream and sage palette + cinematic, slightly muted (reads under white captions) --oref <url> --ar 9:16 --sref 2013954648 293616715 --sw 120 --stylize 150 --no text, logos, watermark, ui`

Rules: **9:16 native** (no cropping issues); compose with the subject off-center or lower-third so the vertical middle stays readable; faces → `--oref` (character canon); the template darkens them, so lean slightly muted/cinematic, not bright.

**Stills OR motion — a beat `bg` accepts a `.png` (Ken-Burns) or a `.mp4` (live motion).** Midjourney does image-to-video, and *real* motion beats a digital zoom for stopping the scroll — animate at least the **hook beat** (steam rising, lamplight flicker, hair/curtain movement, the phone-glow pulsing). Pipeline: generate the still → animate it in MJ (image-to-video) → download the clip → drop the `.mp4` in `content-studio/public/` → set that beat's `bg` to the `.mp4`. Keep clips short/light; the template only uses ~1.5s of each. (GlowReel also takes an MJ video loop via its `isVideo` flag for the Feeling posts.) Motion verbs to add when prompting the animation: "gentle, subtle, slow, ambient — light shifts, steam drifts, a slight breeze," never fast or jumpy.

**Worked example — the mental-load hook video (3 beats):**
1. *pain:* tired mother at a cluttered counter, flyers/sticky-notes on the fridge, phone in hand, weary — subject lower-third.
2. *aha:* a parent photographing a school flyer, soft magic glow around the phone.
3. *relief:* a family of four relaxed together at the kitchen table, warm lamplight.

Workflow: write the beat script → generate one scene per beat → drop in `content-studio/public/` → set each beat's `bg` in `src/Root.tsx` → `npm run render` (HookReel). New story = new scenes + new caption lines.
