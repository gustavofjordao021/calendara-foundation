# Start here 👋

Welcome to Calendara's **Brand & Growth HQ** — where everything about how Calendara *looks, sounds, and shows up* lives. You don't need to be technical to use it.

## The 1-minute version
- We help families **coordinate so they can be together.** Tone: warm, calm, unpretentious — never corporate. Colors: coral, cream, sage. *(Full detail in [`brand-book.md`](brand-book.md).)*
- Our picture style is **"Storybook Glow"** — warm, painterly, family-together scenes. Recipes to make images (Midjourney + logo insertion) are in [`image-system/`](image-system/).

## Where things are
- **Words & decisions** → right here in GitHub. To edit any `.md` file in your browser: open it → click the ✏️ pencil (top-right) → type → **"Commit changes"** at the bottom. That's it — no install, no terminal.
- **Images & video** → the **Google Drive library** (link in [`README.md`](README.md)). Drop new art there; paste the link back into the relevant doc.
- **What we're shipping** → [`content-calendar.md`](content-calendar.md).

## How to get help
Ask **Claude in Cowork** in plain English:
- *"Give me 5 Instagram captions in our voice for the Storybook Glow hero."*
- *"Add this Midjourney prompt to the image system."*
- *"Log this campaign in the content calendar."*
- *"Make 3 hero images for the autumn push."*

Claude edits these files for you and can pull art from Drive.

## Two rules
1. **Keep it warm and on-palette** (coral / cream / sage).
2. **Original art only** — never other companies' characters or logos.

That's everything. Poke around, and ask Claude anything.
