# APPENDIX — Hygge voice rewrite (April 2026)

> Not the live copy yet. This is the rewrite proposal to ship as part of the NOW "Brand voice refresh" item in the [April 2026 roadmap](../roadmap/product-roadmap-april-2026.md). It's a before/after for the page's highest-weight copy blocks, written in the Hygge-tone (warm, present, calm — no productivity verbs). Full-page pass to follow once the direction is approved.

> **Guiding rule:** Strip verbs like *optimize, manage, streamline, reduce, track*. Prefer sensory and temporal nouns — *basket, tuck, evening, candle, table*. Read every line against a single question: would you say this to a friend sitting across from you in their kitchen? If no, rewrite.

## What to keep from the current copy

- The four-card "Sound Familiar?" structure. The *content* of the problems is still right — the *voice* just softens.
- The "Take a photo of…" / "It becomes…" table in Section 4. Functional; Hygge-neutral. Works as-is.
- The step-by-step "How It Works." Structure good; language softens (e.g., "AI extracts it instantly" → "Calendara reads it and tucks it away").
- The Cozi comparison table. Keep the table; rewrite the surrounding narrative to stop leading with price and lead with voice instead.

## What to cut

- "$2.99/mo" everywhere → **$5/mo household plan (one payer, up to 5 people).** Pricing is live; landing page is stale.
- "Everything in Cozi Max. Half the price." This positions us as a discount Cozi, not as Hygge.
- "Organized. Shared. Off your mind." Fine as a reduction; reads like productivity SaaS.
- Alt title: *"Calendara | The Cozi Alternative with AI Superpowers"* — retire. "Superpowers" is exactly the productivity-tone we're moving away from.
